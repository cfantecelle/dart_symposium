    <h1 class="text-4xl font-extrabold tracking-tight">
      About the Symposium
    </h1>

    <p class="mt-6 text-lg leading-relaxed">
      The <b>Symposium on Immunopathology of Tropical Diseases</b> will bring together researchers, clinicians, and public health professionals dedicated to advancing the understanding of diseases that affect tropical and subtropical regions. The event will provide a platform for the exchange of ideas and the presentation of recent findings that contribute to a deeper understanding of the biological, ecological, and social dimensions of tropical diseases.
    </p>

    <p class="mt-6 text-lg leading-relaxed">
      Through roundtable lectures and thematic sessions, participants will explore current research on disease dynamics, host–pathogen interactions, and the factors that influence transmission and disease outcomes. Presentations will highlight interdisciplinary approaches that integrate epidemiology, immunopathology, molecular biology, and emerging research technologies.
    </p>

    <p class="mt-6 text-lg leading-relaxed">
      The symposium will also foster collaboration among scientists working at different scales of investigation, from molecular mechanisms to population-level processes. By bringing together diverse perspectives, the meeting will encourage new partnerships and promote innovative strategies for research and control of tropical diseases.
    </p>
