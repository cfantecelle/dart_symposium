<section class="bg-background py-16">
  <div class="mx-auto max-w-3xl px-4 text-dark text-justified">
    <h1 class="text-4xl font-extrabold tracking-tight">
      Abstract Submission
    </h1>
    <p class="mt-6 text-lg leading-relaxed">
      The <b>1st Symposium on Immunopathology of Tropical Diseases</b> is pleased to invite abstract submissions. We aim to bring together researchers, clinicians, and students to present original findings and discuss emerging concepts related to host–pathogen interactions, disease mechanisms, and translational approaches in tropical diseases. The submissions will be reviewed by the Scientific Committee based on originality,
      relevance, and scientific quality.
    </p>
    <div class="mt-10 space-y-6 leading-relaxed">
      <div>
        <h2 class="text-xl font-semibold">General Guidelines</h2>
        <ul class="mt-3 list-disc pl-5 space-y-2">
          <li>Abstracts must present original research related to the theme of the symposium: <i>Immunopathology of Tropical Diseases</i>.</li>
          <li>Abstracts that consist solely of literature reviews will not be considered.</li>
          <li>All abstracts must be written in <b>English</b>.</li>
          <li>Submissions containing significant language or formatting issues may be rejected.</li>
          <li>The submission deadline is <b>TBD</b>.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div>
        <h2 class="text-xl font-semibold">Review and Selection</h2>
        All submitted abstracts will be evaluated by the <b>Scientific Committee</b> based on:
        <ul class="mt-3 list-disc pl-5 space-y-2">
          <li>Scientific relevance to the symposium theme.</li>
          <li>Originality and innovation.</li>
          <li>Methodological quality.</li>
          <li>Clarity and organisation.</li>
        </ul>
        Based on this evaluation, abstracts will be selected for <b>Poster Presentation</b>. The selected authors will be notified by e-mail.
      </div>
      <div>
        <h2 class="text-xl font-semibold">Submission Rules</h2>
        <ul class="mt-3 list-disc pl-5 space-y-2">
          <li>Each presenting author may submit <b>only one abstract</b>.</li>
          <li>The presenting author must be <b>registered for the symposium</b>.</li>
          <li>The presenting author is expected to present the work (poster).</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div>
        <h2 class="text-xl font-semibold">Submission Process</h2>
        Abstracts must be submitted using the form at the end of this page.
        During the submission, the authors will be asked to provide:
        <ul class="mt-3 list-disc pl-5 space-y-2">
          <li>Full name of the <i>submitting</i> author (must match the name used during registration).</li>
          <li>Preferred e-mail for contact.</li>
          <li>Title of the abstract.</li>
          <li>Name of the <i>presenting</i> author.</li>
          <li>A <b>.pdf</b> file containing the abstract.</li>
        </ul>
        <br>We advise all authors to please review all information carefully before final submission.
      </div>
      <div>
        <h2 class="text-xl font-semibold">Abstract Guidelines</h2>
        <ul class="mt-3 list-disc pl-5 space-y-2">
          <li><b>Title:</b> maximum 255 characters (including spaces).</li>
          <li><b>Text:</b> maximum 2,000 characters (including spaces).</li>
          <li><b>Language:</b> English.</li>
          <li><b>Keywords:</b> 3 keywords must be included (at the end of text).</li>
          <li><b>Abstracts must contain:</b> background, objectives, methods, results and conclusions. However, the text must be written continuously, without naming each section.</li>
          <li><b>Abbreviations:</b> defined at first use.</li>
          <li><b>Do <i>not</i> include:</b> Tables, figures or images.</li>          
        </ul>        
      </div>
      <div>
        <h2 class="text-xl font-semibold">Abstract File Structure</h2>
        Abstracts must be saved as .pdf files and constructed using the following rules:
        <ul class="mt-3 list-disc pl-5 space-y-2">
          <li><b>Overall structure:</b>
            <ul class="mt-3 list-disc pl-5 space-y-2">
              <li>Title (do not use all uppercase letters).</li>
              <li>Authors.</li>
              <li>Line break.</li>
              <li>Author affiliations.</li>
              <li>Line break.</li>
              <li>Abstract text.</li>
              <li>Line break.</li>
              <li>Keywords.</li>
            </ul>
          </li>
          <li><b>Text Formatting:</b>
            <ul class="mt-3 list-disc pl-5 space-y-2">
              <li><b>Margins:</b> 2cm on all sides.</li>
              <li><b>Font:</b> Arial</li>
              <li><b>Font size:</b> 12. </li>
              <li><b>Titles:</b> Bold.</li>
              <li><b>Authors and affiliations font size:</b> 10.</li>
            </ul>
          </li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div>
        <h2 class="text-xl font-semibold">Poster Presentation</h2>        
        <ul class="mt-3 list-disc pl-5 space-y-2">
          <li>Posters must be <i>written and presented</i> in <b>English</b>.</li>
          <li><b>Dimensions:</b> 0.90 m (width) × 1.20 m (height).</li>
          <li>The title must match the accepted abstract.</li>
          <li><b>Posters must include:</b> Objectives, Methods, Results, and Conclusions.</li>
          <li>The presenting author must be present during the assigned session.</li>
        </ul>
        <br>Poster numbers and presentation details will be provided in advance.
      </div>
      <div>
        <h2 class="text-xl font-semibold">Poster Evaluation</h2>        
        Poster presentations will be evaluated by members of the Scientific Committee based on:
        <ul class="mt-3 list-disc pl-5 space-y-2">
          <li>Relevance to the symposium theme.</li>
          <li>Scientific quality.</li>
          <li>Clarity and organization.</li>
          <li>Originality.</li>
          <li>Impact of findings.</li>
          <li>Oral presentation.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div>
        <h2 class="text-xl font-semibold">Registration</h2>        
        All presenting authors must complete registration through the symposium website.<br>
        Participants are encouraged to register before the submission deadline to ensure eligibility.
      </div>
